Gosaikunda Trek

$60.00 per guest

Langtang, Nepal

The expedition begins with a six-hour drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi. To reach Yala Peak, climbers will need to first follow the Langtang Valley Trek to Kyanjin Gompa on a three-day trek. From there, there are a few acclimatization day-hikes such as Kyanjin Ri and Tserko Ri. Once Acclimatized, climbers have a two-day expedition to climb Yala Peak. A two-day trek back out along the Langtang Valley Trek completes the expedition. The expedition finishes with a six-hour drive from Syabrubesi to Kathmandu.

Distance: 18km return trip from Kyanjin Gompa

Days required: 2 days with overnight stay at Yala Kharka Basecamp
